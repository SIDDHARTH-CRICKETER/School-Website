  • Heathcare and Safety!

    A doctor is in attendance every day who looks after the specific needs of students who may be ill or who may need attention periodically throughout the day. In case the child suffers from any medical ailment or illness, parents are requested to give detailed instructions regarding first aid for any special medical problems e.g. diabetes, asthma or allergies. The students receiving transient or long-term medical treatment are requested to discuss this with the school Doctor prior to attending school and the appropriate information should be documented on the medical form. Self-medication will not be allowed. At SIS every student and staff is covered under a Medical Insurance from the date of joining the School and we conduct Annual Health Check-ups for students and staff by Symbiosis Centre for Healthcare, Pune.

  • Eats @ SIS

    SIS is very much a ‘Healthy Body, Healthy Mind’ school. All our food is freshly prepared on-site to provide the highest nutritional value. This also allows us to meet a range of dietary requirements. Each day the school receives delivery of locally-sourced, healthy organic produce. Our menu also changes daily, so students experience a range of foods that will fulfil their nutritional needs whilst at school. This includes breakfast in the morning, lunch in the afternoon and evening snacks. We believe that great food promotes motivation and focus for better learning. The monthly food menu is shared with the parents through our school software.
